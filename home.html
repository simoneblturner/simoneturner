My name is Simone Turner and I am a current junior majoring in Data Analytics in the School of Information at University of Michigan. While I am a data science major, I am studying the intersection between people, information, and technology. This allows me to tackle problems through a wider lens and strive for solutions in a more socially responsible way. In order to further inform my approach to studying data and technology, I am minoring in Political Science. I am passionate about the growing data industry in the infinite applications and uses of big data in every industry today. I thrive when I work through problems on a close knit team. In one of my classes this semester I am building a database in a small group using sql for the big ten schools. Even though we are working virtually, bouncing ideas off one another on Zoom in real time is the most efficient and effective way to build our final product. I enjoy talking through my code and bouncing ideas off my peers.
